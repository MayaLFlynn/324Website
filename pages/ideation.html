Ideation

Identify relevant artifacts from the human-centered design process that address the following points.
What techniques did you use to discover solutions to users' needs?
What solution(s) did you ultimate pursue and why did you choose them?

We created personas in order to think about what users may want and need. The three personas that we came up with were: Ethon, Alfredo Dubois, and Marigold. We added aspects to each of them that would help us create an artifact that worked well for many times of people. Some of the characteristics that we added to our personas were: colorblind, tech savvy, sentimental, not tech savvy, not observant, chaotic, and minmaxer.

These personas guided how we thought about designing our game, from making sure that the color contrast was high for accessibility to chosing where we led the story. Personas were a good way for us to design our game for specific people, without those specific people being ourselves. Since we made our personas different, we were able create a design that fits a broad audience, without having to think about more than a few people.
